# IT Setup Guide

Welcome to the IT setup guide for new employees. This document covers everything you need to get your workstation up and running on your first day.

## VPN Access

To request VPN access, submit a ticket on the IT Service Portal under "Network Access". Select "VPN — New User" and provide your employee ID and manager's name. Approval typically takes 24 hours. Once approved, download the GlobalProtect VPN client from the internal software catalog. Use your corporate email and temporary password to log in. You must complete MFA enrollment through the Okta dashboard before your first VPN connection.

## Laptop Configuration

All employees receive a pre-configured laptop from IT on day one. The standard image includes Windows 11 or macOS Sonoma, Microsoft Office 365, Slack, Zoom, and Chrome. If you need additional software, submit a request through the IT Service Portal under "Software Request". Admin-level installations require manager approval and take 48 hours to process. Do not install unapproved software — it will be flagged by the endpoint monitoring agent.

## Software Access Request Process

To request access to internal tools such as Jira, Confluence, GitHub Enterprise, or AWS Console, go to the IT Service Portal and select "Application Access". Fill in the application name, your team, and the level of access needed (read-only, contributor, or admin). Each request is routed to the application owner for approval. Standard access is granted within one business day. Admin-level access requires VP approval and may take up to three business days.
