# HR Policy Handbook

This document outlines key HR policies that apply to all full-time employees. Please review this during your first week and reach out to hr-support@company.com with any questions.

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year, accrued monthly. Sick leave is separate and provides 12 days annually. To request leave, submit a request in the HR Portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned leave. Emergency or sick leave can be reported on the same day by notifying your manager via Slack and logging it in the portal within 48 hours. Unused PTO can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year. Any excess is forfeited on January 31st.

## Work From Home Policy

Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Core in-office days are Tuesday and Thursday. WFH requests should be logged in the HR Portal by the prior Friday. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and a signed remote work agreement. All remote workers must ensure a stable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps and a dedicated workspace. Equipment stipends of $500 are available for first-time remote setup — submit a claim under "WFH Equipment" in the expense portal.

## Expense Claims

Business-related expenses can be claimed through the Expense Portal. Attach receipts for all claims over $25. Meals during business travel are capped at $75 per day. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Manager approval is required for all claims. Reimbursement is processed in the next payroll cycle, typically within 15 business days. For international travel expenses, convert to USD using the exchange rate on the date of the transaction.
